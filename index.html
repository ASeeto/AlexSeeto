Web Developer

Web development has been a passion of mine for as long as I can remember. I love being able to create, design, and contribute to useful applications that are easy for others to understand as well as visually appealing. I started out learning mostly front end, but have been developing closer towards the full stack. I've worked on various projects for school and work and have some independent projects of my own. Software Engineer

I aspire to develop software that will make a difference in the world. Graphic design is another creative outlet for me and I liked being able to contribute to my organizations while at the same time learning, practicing, and refining new skills.
